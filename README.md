## ✨ What relvio does

relvio is a local-first personal CRM for Windows. It builds your contact list from Gmail and keeps all data on your machine.

Use it to:

- collect people from your email history
- keep notes on contacts
- find past conversations fast
- track relationship details in one place
- keep your data off cloud services

## 📥 How the import works

relvio reads your Gmail history and builds a contact list from people you already know.

It looks for:

- people you email often
- people who email you
- names in message threads
- common contact details in your mail

It then groups that information into contact records so you can keep track of each person in one place.

## 🧱 What you need

relvio is made for a Windows desktop computer.

A good setup includes:

- Windows 10 or Windows 11
- a Gmail account
- internet access for the first sign-in and import
- enough free space for your email data and local database

For best results, use the same Gmail account you already use for work or personal messages.

4. restore the copy if you move to a new PC

## ❓ Common questions

### Is relvio a cloud app?

No. relvio keeps your data on your computer.

### Do I need a subscription?

No. You run it on your own machine.

### Can I use it with more than one Gmail account?

You can use it with the Gmail account that holds the contacts you want to track. If you work from several inboxes, choose the one with the most useful history first.

### Does it replace my email app?

No. It works beside your email. It helps you turn message history into a contact list.

### Can I move my data to a new PC?

Yes. Copy the local data folder and restore it on the other machine.

## 📌 Best results

To get the most from relvio:

- use one main Gmail account
- keep your inbox searchable
- add notes after key calls and meetings
- review imported contacts each week
- back up your local database on a schedule
