Import LinkedIn Contacts

How to export your LinkedIn connections:

  1. Go to LinkedIn.com → Me → Settings & Privacy
  2. Click Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data
  3. Select Connections only → Request archive
  4. LinkedIn will email you a download link (usually within minutes)
  5. Download the ZIP, extract it, and find Connections.csv
